A quiet public surface for serious work.

Toly is not presenting a finished catalogue. This site exists to say what is true now: we are shaping the company, testing ideas privately, and keeping the public record accurate while the work matures.

The near-term focus is discipline: clear decisions, small proofs, useful constraints, and a product standard that does not depend on hype. When a Toly project becomes public, it should arrive with a clear purpose, a plain explanation, and realistic expectations.

What guides the work.

These are operating principles, not claims of achievement. They are here so the public tone stays aligned with how the company is being formed.

Useful before impressive

We are interested in tools and experiences that solve real problems or create clear value, not surfaces built only to look busy.

Honest by default

If something is experimental, private, incomplete, or not yet available, we will say so plainly.

Built to be maintained

We want releases that can be supported responsibly, documented clearly, and improved without confusing the people who rely on them.

How public releases will appear.

The website will stay modest until there is something concrete to share. That restraint is part of the trust we want to build.
